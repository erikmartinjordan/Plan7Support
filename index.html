Managing your subscription and getting the most out of Plan7.

FAQ

How do I subscribe?

Open Plan7, go to sharing, and tap upgrade. Choose Monthly or Yearly, then confirm via the App Store.

How do I cancel?

Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Plan7 → Cancel.

I cancelled. Why do I still have access?

Your subscription stays active until the current billing period ends. Premium features lock after that.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the upgrade screen in Plan7 and tap Restore Purchases.

Family Sharing?

Not yet supported. Each member needs their own subscription.

Privacy Policy

July 17, 2026

1. What we collect

  • Account data — a unique ID so shared plans sync between your devices and other participants.
  • Anonymous usage — which features you use, so we know what to improve. Not tied to your identity.
  • Subscription status — a simple yes/no flag. We never see your payment details — Apple handles all of that.

2. How we use it

  • To make the app work (meal plans, shared spaces, syncing).
  • To sync across your devices via iCloud when you enable it.
  • To unlock premium features if you're paying.
  • To figure out what to build next, based on anonymous usage patterns.

3. Where your data lives

On your device, and optionally in your private iCloud container. We don't run servers that hold your data.

4. Third parties

None. No analytics SDKs, no ad networks, no trackers. Purchases go through Apple's StoreKit — we never touch your payment info.

5. Data retention

Delete the app, your local data is gone. Shared plans disappear when everyone leaves. For anything on our side, just ask.

6. Kids

Plan7 isn't aimed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect their data.

7. Changes

If this policy changes, we'll post the update here. Using the app after that means you're cool with it.
