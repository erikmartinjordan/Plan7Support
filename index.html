Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe?

Open Plan7, navigate to the sharing screen, and tap the upgrade button. Choose between Monthly and Yearly plans, then confirm your purchase through the App Store.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions. Select Plan7 and choose Cancel Subscription.

I cancelled but still have access

Your subscription stays active until the current billing period ends. After that, premium features will be locked.

How do I restore a previous purchase?

Open Plan7, go to the upgrade screen, and tap Restore Purchases. Your subscription will be restored automatically.

Can I share my subscription with family?

Family Sharing is not supported at this time. Each user needs their own subscription.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Plan7 collects minimal data to provide and improve our service:

  • Account data: When you create shared plans, we store a unique identifier so the app can sync data between your devices and with other participants.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics about how you use the app (features used, screens visited) to help us improve the experience. This does not personally identify you.
  • Purchase data: We do not collect or store payment information. All purchases are securely processed by Apple via StoreKit. We only receive a boolean indicating whether you have an active subscription.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality (meal plans, shared spaces).
  • To sync data across your devices via iCloud when enabled.
  • To unlock premium features for paying subscribers.
  • To improve the app through anonymous analytics.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and optionally in your private iCloud container. We do not operate our own servers. We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data.

4. Third-Party Services

Plan7 does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. All purchases go through Apple's StoreKit system — we never have access to your payment details.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Data is retained only as long as necessary. You can delete the app to remove local data. Shared plan data is deleted when all participants leave. To request deletion of server-side logs, contact us.

6. Children's Privacy

Plan7 is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
